Step

A Step

A step shows the completion status of an activity in a series of activities.

Shipping
Choose your shipping options

Usage :
- A step is required to put into a steps block that is generated with a <div> element and classes .ui, .steps.
- Include a class .step with a <div> element. 


<div class="ui steps">
  <div class="step">
    ...
  </div>
</div>

Description

A step can contain a description.

Shipping
Choose your shipping options

Usage : include title and description divs to the step. 


<div class="ui steps">
  <div class="step">
    <div class="title">Shipping</div>
    <div class="description">Choose your shipping options</div>
  </div>
</div>

Icon

A step can contain an icon.

Shipping
Choose your shipping options

Usage : append an <i> element with an icon to the content. 


<div class="ui steps">
  <div class="step">
    <!-- Icon -->
    <i class="paper plane icon"></i>
    <div class="content">
      <div class="title">Shipping</div>
      <div class="description">Choose your shipping options</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
